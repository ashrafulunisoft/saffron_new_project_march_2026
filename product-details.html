About Premium Roshogolla

Our Premium Roshogolla is the crown jewel of Bengali sweets. Each roshogolla is carefully crafted using traditional methods that have been perfected over generations.

The process begins with fresh cow's milk, which is curdled to create the perfect chhana (cottage cheese). This chhana is then kneaded by hand until it reaches a silky smooth consistency, shaped into perfect spheres, and boiled in a delicate sugar syrup infused with cardamom and saffron.

Why Choose Our Roshogolla?
  • Made with 100% pure cow's milk
  • No artificial colors or preservatives
  • Traditional hand-kneaded preparation
  • Perfect texture - soft, spongy, and juicy
  • Balanced sweetness that enhances the milk flavor
  • Packaged hygienically to maintain freshness

Fresh Cow's Milk (Chhana/Cottage Cheese)
